The Merrimack Historical Society has many resources for researchers of history and genealogy within the town of Merrimack, New Hampshire, and the surrounding region including:

  • Collections (artifacts, documents, town histories and genealogies at the historical society)
  • Merrimack Records (on this site) including a list of persons buried in local cemeteries
  • Scrapbook collections donated that include obituaries and other news articles, especially from 1930s through the 1960s.
  • Town reports that include births, marriages and deaths.
  • School teacher and schoolhouse indexes. A significant collection of information and photographs of McGaw Institute graduates.
  • Photograph collections.
  • Genealogical Family files collected over several years.


Most of these resources are on-site at our society building, and must be visited
in person, or requested by email or regular mail.

The following resources which are available now on-line:

  • Virtual Library (stories, genealogies, Walking Tour of Merrimack NH brochure)
  • Photographs (old and new)
  • People of Note
