Photographs of Merrimack, New Hampshire and artifacts in the collection of the Merrimack Historical Society


The Merrimack Historical Society has a collection of photographs which are a dramatic way to preserve our history--"how we used to look"--for future generations. We always welcome donations of photographs, albums, and other types of both visual and audio media, relating to our town's history.
