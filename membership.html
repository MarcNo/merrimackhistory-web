CATEGORIES OF MEMBERSHIP & DUES

- Single membership $15 / year
- Family membership (two persons from same household) - $25 / year
- Senior/Student membership (Seniors 60 and older) - $10 / year
- Life Membership (individuals only) - $150.00 / year (under age 60); $100.00 / year (60 and older)
- Business Membership $50.00 / year


Dues are payable yearly in October for the upcoming year. Also consider making a gift of membership to the history buff in your family.

BENEFITS

DISCOVER ....
your family history... and meet your neighbors... and uncover Merrimack's connections to significant people and events...

PARTICIPATE....
in new activities... and become part of Merrimack's current history... and preserve Merrimack's past TODAY...

EXPLORE...
our "Virtual Library"... research your family... learn more about your house and neighbors... see glimpses of the past through photographs...

SUPPORT...
behind the scenes documentation of Merrimack's past and present... preservation of the museum's collections... research... cataloguing... curriculum projects

A yearly program book is distributed to local members at the time of subscription. Program books are mailed out only to life members.
